About Us

Bridging EU and the MENA Region

AssoMENA is a non-profit association founded in 2014 that serves as a bridge between EU and the Middle East & North Africa, fostering a flow of capital, skills, technologies, and culture for mutual growth.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

We connect European small and medium enterprises with the MENA region through a structured platform that facilitates trade, partnerships, and cross-border opportunities. Our mission is to strengthen economic ties, promote cultural exchange, and empower businesses to discover new markets — grounded in collaboration, shared knowledge, and mutual growth.

Business Facilitation

We facilitate introductions, trade leads, and B2B matchmaking between European enterprises and MENA markets across more than 50 countries.

SME Support

We provide dedicated support to small and medium industrial enterprises, helping them navigate international markets and access growth opportunities.

Innovation & Research

We promote innovation through access to events, seminars, and research initiatives that drive sustainable business development.

Cultural Exchange

We foster cultural understanding between EU and the MENA region, building trust and long-lasting business relationships.

European Visibility

We enhance the visibility of European products, services, and expertise across Middle Eastern and North African markets.

Market Entry

We assist businesses with strategic market entry into the MENA region, providing guidance, local contacts, and operational support.

Ramez Nouaimeh

CEO & Founder

Ramez Nouaimeh is a visionary entrepreneur with deep Lebanese roots and a strong legacy in the infrastructure sector. Coming from the renowned Tajj Group, a family of successful entrepreneurs, he has built a reputation for bridging European businesses with thriving markets in the Middle East and North Africa.

Since 1998, Ramez has played a pivotal role in facilitating European companies' entry into MENA markets, fostering strategic partnerships that drive international expansion. With an extensive network spanning Beirut, Dubai, Saudi Arabia, and beyond, he has helped numerous businesses navigate new territories, unlock growth potential, and establish a strong presence in some of the world's most dynamic economies.

As the Founder of AssoMENA, Ramez continues to serve as a key connector between EU and the MENA region, offering expert consultancy to help companies overcome cultural, regulatory, and business challenges. With Ramez and his expert team, businesses gain market access and secure a strategic, results-driven approach to international expansion.
