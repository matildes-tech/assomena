01

European Companies

Expand your business into MENA markets. Access trade leads, find local partners, and attend B2B events tailored to your industry.

02

Startups & Entrepreneurs

Get visibility, connect with investors and mentors, and discover opportunities to scale your innovation across borders.

03

Why Join AssoMENA?

From verified business connections to AI-powered tools, here is what our members get access to.

Verified Business Network

Connect with pre-vetted European companies and MENA-based partners. Every company on our platform goes through a verification process to ensure quality connections.

B2B Matchmaking

Our intelligent matchmaking engine analyses your profile, sector, and goals to surface the most relevant partners, saving you hours of manual research.

Trade Opportunities

Browse and post trade leads across import, export, distribution, and joint ventures. Reach decision-makers in the EU-MENA corridor directly.

Events & Networking

Attend exclusive B2B events, trade fairs, and webinars. Meet potential partners face-to-face or virtually, and build relationships that drive real business.

AI-Powered Tools

Use our built-in AI assistant to get market insights, draft outreach messages, and analyse opportunities — all within your dashboard.

Membership & Visibility

Boost your company profile in search results, showcase products in the marketplace, and gain priority access to premium opportunities.
